Treatment FAQs.

+ How do I know the clinic I am searching is verified?

Clinics are verified through several steps:

  • Accreditation by recognized medical bodies.
  • Reviews and testimonials from previous patients.
  • Inspection and certification by healthcare authorities.
  • Consistent track record of successful treatments.
  • Transparent communication and professional conduct.
+ I can't travel alone so can some family member or friend of mine accompany me?

Yes, most clinics and treatment centers allow family members or friends to accompany you during your travel and treatment period.

+ Can I see my doctor before I decide and what about my post recovery?

Yes, pre and post treatment consultation can be done. You can speak to the doctor at the most appropriate time (before you decide to travel) to boost your confidence. The team at Mediretreats will also help you with arranging the OPD’S after the surgeries.

+ How do I share my reports and medical cases with the clinic of my choice?

You can share your reports and medical cases through secure online platforms provided by the clinic, or via email after ensuring the privacy and confidentiality measures are in place.
